Water Storage

✓Store 1 gallon per person per day for 2 weeks (approx. 15 gallons/person)

✓Consider storing water in food-grade containers and keeping them in a cool, dark place to prevent contamination.

Food Storage

✓ Store non-perishable food items for at least 2 weeks

✓ Don't forget to rotate your food supplies regularly to ensure freshness.

  • Canned goods
  • Dry grains and pasta
  • Freeze-dried meals
